This app collects location related info (such as but not limited to speed, direction, and accuracy). These data can be shared with other users without any indication to the user or device that were collected from. 
The app uses Firebase Cloud Notifications. The privacy policy can be found at: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
The app is using crashlytics for crash reporting. Crashlytics privacy policy is: http://try.crashlytics.com/terms/
